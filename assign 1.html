scrambeled eggs

Eggs are one of my favourite foods.here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambeled eggs

Ingrdients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. once butter has melted add cream eggs
  4. using a spatula the eggs from the edge of the pan to the centre every 20 secs
  5. when the eggs are still moist remove from the heat
